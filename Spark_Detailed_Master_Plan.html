The Core Concept

To legally replicate the Jarsy model, Spark cannot be a single company. It must be a structure. You are building a "Legal Wrapper" around pre-IPO equity.

1. The Brain (USA): Builds the tech, owns the brand.
2. The Vault (Cayman): Holds the SpaceX shares.
3. The Face (BVI): Sells the tokens to the world.
