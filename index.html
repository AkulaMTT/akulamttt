Core Planning Features

Dynamic Profit Targeting

Set your profit goal "Per Day," "Per Month," or "Per Year" and watch the entire dashboard update instantly.

Dual-Currency System

Seamlessly manage finances in USD and a second, user-selectable currency (e.g., LYD, TRY, EUR, SAR).

Bidirectional Currency Inputs

Enter a cost in USD to see its secondary equivalent, or enter in your secondary currency to see the USD value.

Granular Per-Unit Costing

Model every component of your product cost, including Cost of Goods, Packaging, Shipping, and Exporting.

Flexible Cost Allocation

Instantly model "Customer pays for Shipping" scenarios by simply checking a box, removing that cost from your calculation.

Fixed & Variable Overheads

Account for all monthly expenses, from fixed website domains to variable marketing budgets on Facebook, Google, and TikTok.

AI-Powered Sourcing & Analysis Powered by Gemini

AI Product Sourcing

Don't know your costs? Just enter a "Product Search Term." The AI will use Google Search to find real, current wholesale pricing from sites like Alibaba and DHGate.

It presents a list of options with estimated costs and source links. You can then click "Select" to automatically populate the "Cost of Goods" field in the calculator.

AI Pricing & Margin Analysis

Already have your product? Enter your product, cost, and selling price. The AI will perform an in-depth analysis of your profit margin and health.

It provides expert commentary, suggests three alternative pricing strategies (e.g., Bundle, Decoy), and even generates a compelling product description for your store.
